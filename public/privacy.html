Privacy Overview

No Tracking No Personal Data Sale Encrypted

At Search Engine, we believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our search services.

We've designed our search engine from the ground up with privacy in mind. We don't track you, we don't profile you, and we don't sell your data. Ever.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our search service:

Search Queries

When you search, we temporarily process your query to deliver results. Search queries are:

  • Not associated with your identity
  • Not stored permanently
  • Deleted from our servers within 24 hours
  • Never used for advertising or profiling

Technical Information

Data Type Purpose Retention
IP Address (anonymized) Prevent abuse, regional results Never stored in full
Browser Type Optimize display Session only
Language Preference Deliver localized results Session only

How We Use Data

We use the minimal data we collect exclusively to:

  • Deliver search results: Process your query and return relevant results
  • Improve our service: Analyze aggregated, anonymous usage patterns
  • Prevent abuse: Detect and prevent automated attacks or misuse
  • Legal compliance: Respond to valid legal requests when required

What We DON'T Do

  • Create user profiles
  • Track your searches over time
  • Sell or share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing
  • Store your personal information

Data Protection

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for all searches
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Strict access controls and authentication

Organizational Measures

  • Privacy by design in all our systems
  • Regular staff training on data protection
  • Data minimization principles
  • Incident response procedures

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Your Privacy Rights Include:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data we process
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Team using the information provided below.

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for essential functionality:

Essential Cookies

  • Session cookies: Maintain your preferences during a search session
  • Security cookies: Protect against cross-site request forgery
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language and region settings

No Tracking Cookies

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media cookies
  • Third-party tracking cookies

Third Parties

We limit third-party access to protect your privacy:

Service Providers

We work with carefully selected service providers who:

  • Are contractually bound to protect your data
  • Only process data on our instructions
  • Cannot use your data for their own purposes
  • Meet our strict security standards

Legal Requests

We may disclose data only when legally required and will:

  • Challenge overly broad requests
  • Notify users when legally permitted
  • Provide only the minimum data required
  • Publish transparency reports
